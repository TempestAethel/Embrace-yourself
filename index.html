A love unspoken, trapped within my heart,
A fear of past wounds, a haunting blight,
I long to confess, to play my chosen part,
Yet doubt and fear, extinguish hope and light.

A future dreamed of, filled with love and grace,
A world where flaws are seen, where faults are known,
Yet reality, a harsh, unyielding place,
Where hope is shattered, leaving me alone.

I dare not speak, my love, a silent plea,
Afraid of rejection, of scorn and pain,
A future out of reach, a distant sea,
A love unrequited, forever in vain.
Oh, cruel fate, that mocks my every plea,
A love unspoken, destined to be free.

A heart consumed by sorrow, deep and wide,
A soul weighed down by doubt and despair,
I long to weep, to let my anguish guide,
A life of torment, beyond all repair.

Words once cherished, now a heavy load,
Their weight unchecked, a crushing, cruel blow,
I'm lost within, a solitary road,
Gaslighted, deceived, where shadows grow.

Confusion reigns, as truth eludes my grasp,
I seek for trust, for love, for understanding,
Yet doubt and fear, my spirit overcast,
A wounded soul, forever wandering.

Oh, cruel fate, that mocks my every plea,
A life of turmoil, destined to be free.
